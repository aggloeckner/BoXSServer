waitForExperimenter()

Sometimes the experimenter needs the experiment to wait until he has done something, e.g. explaining the next stage to the subjects. The waitForExperimenter-command halts the execution of the experiment for all subjects until the experimenter presses the "Ready"-button.

Parameters

No Parameters

  • waitForPlayers
  • waitTime

Examples

Standard usage

waitForExperimenter()
(no output is shown for the subjects)
